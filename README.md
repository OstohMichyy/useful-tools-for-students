# Useful-tools-for-students

This repository collects tools that help students with productivity and learning.


## Notion

Notion is an all-in-one digital workspace that helps students organize notes, tasks, and projects in one place.  
It combines the flexibility of a notebook with the structure of a planner, making it ideal for both individual study and group collaboration.

### Key Features
-  **Note-taking**: Create structured notes with headings, lists, tables, and embedded media.
-  **Task management**: Track assignments and deadlines using to-do lists, calendars, or Kanban boards.
-  **Collaboration**: Share pages with classmates and work together in real time.
-  **Search & Organization**: Quickly find notes and resources across your workspace.
-  **Templates**: Use pre-built templates for planners, study dashboards, or project trackers.
-  **Notion AI**: Summarize notes, generate flashcards, or get quick explanations to boost productivity.

### Why Students Love It
- Keeps everything in one place (notes, tasks, schedules, projects).
- Easy to customize with templates for different subjects or clubs.
- Works seamlessly across devices (desktop, mobile, web).
- Free plan available for students, with premium features at a discount.

### Example Use Cases
-  **Student Planner**: Organize classes, assignments, and exams in a single dashboard.
-  **Study Dashboard**: Track progress across subjects and create revision notes.
-  **Group Projects**: Collaborate with teammates by sharing pages and assigning tasks.
-  **Career Prep**: Manage job applications, resumes, and interview notes in one workspace.
